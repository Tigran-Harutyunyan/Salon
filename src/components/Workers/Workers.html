SENIOR STYLIST

French born Anne Chevallier is a Senior Stylist at Cristophe Salon Beverly Hills. She began her hair career as an apprentice at “l ecole de coiffure de Paris”, while also working for Dessange and Courrèges in Paris.After living in Washington DC, Anne moved to Los Angeles and began her career with Frederic Fekkai. Within her fourteen years at Fekkai, Anne was featured four years in a row for “Best Blowouts in Los Angeles” by Allure Magazine
